About Us

Little Flower High School Kavalam.

Little Flower HS Kavalam, located at Kunnumma is one of the popular schools in India.It is an aided High School in Kuttanadu DEO under the Corporate Management of Archdiocese of Changanacherry. This institution is counted among the top-rated Schools in Kerala with an excellent academic track record.The school started its journey as a lower primary school in 1927.It was upgraded to UP school later and uplifted again to High School in 1984. It has completed its 34th SSLC batch in 2020. Completing its'Navathy'in 2017, the school has completed its preliminaries and is pursuing its higher tasks.Coming days are more beautiful and the coming victories are sweeter.
